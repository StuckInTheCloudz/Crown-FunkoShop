Story of the Sproutling

The Sproutling Funko pop is based on my little sister. The story goes that there was once a little rose plant that sprouted by a little garden in Daly City next to my Grandpa's House. She smelled Fried Chicken fresh from Chinaa Town resting by the window and sprouted out of the ground to steal the whole bowl leaving me with none. Yes this is a true story Instead of getting fed formula she got fed fried chicken by my grandpa.
