About CROWN

CROWN is my first attempt at coding a website specifically designed for ecommerce. My mom and sister like to crochet so I thought i'd help them out by learning a bit of HTML to kick off a website for them. Anyways the goal of CROWN is to provide our customers with an array of Custom built Funko's tailored to what YOU want a figure of. Alot of the time I find FunkoPop collectors wondering howcome there isn't a figure of this or that, so why not create a business that could create the ones you want FOR YOU based on your details and criterias.
