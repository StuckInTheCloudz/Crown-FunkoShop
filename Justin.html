Story of the Dreamer

The Dreamer modeled after my eldest Cousin. The Dreamer gets his name from how he always has a blunt and emotionless expression sometimes almost as if he was daydreaming in the middle of the conversation. One second I'm talking the next he's texting his girlfriend or he fell asleep. None the less Cousin#1 got them dreams so keep following your dreams.
